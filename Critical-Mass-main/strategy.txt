Critical Mass Bot Strategy

Team Name: BOTZILLA

Overview:
Our bot is designed to play Chain Reaction using a dynamic heuristic-based decision-making algorithm.
It does not rely on hardcoded move sequences. Instead, it evaluates the board and simulates future
board states before selecting the best move.

Approach:
1. Valid Move Generation:
   The bot first identifies all legal moves available to the current player.

2. Move Simulation:
   For each valid move, the bot simulates the complete board state after the move,
   including all chain reactions caused by explosions.

3. Heuristic Evaluation:
   Each resulting board is scored based on:
   - Orb count advantage
   - Cell control advantage
   - Corner cell control
   - Near-critical explosion opportunities
   - Risk from dangerous opponent cells

4. Opponent Response Prediction:
   The bot estimates the opponent’s strongest immediate response and factors that
   into the final decision.

5. Final Move Selection:
   The move with the highest final heuristic score is selected.

Design Goals:
- Stay within the 1-second move limit
- Handle chain reactions efficiently
- Play both offensively and defensively
- Remain dynamic and adaptable across different board states

Conclusion:
This bot balances expansion, stability, and tactical chain reactions to make efficient
and competitive decisions in the game.
